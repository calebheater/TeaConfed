What we're all about...

The Tea Confederation is a commercial organization dedicated to the sale and enjoyment of tea. This organization's mission is to provide a relaxing, and aesthetically pleasing environment in which consumers can discover and celebrate the benefits and intricacies of various teas. From healthy green, to soothing chamomile, to luxurious chai, The Tea Confederation has something for everyone. Have fun, and thank you for visiting.
