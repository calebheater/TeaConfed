Why Chamomile Tea?

Unlike the other teas that are listed, chamomile is a floral tea, not an herbal one. Chamomile is a flower known for its soothing and restorative properties. Many people drink chamomile tea before bed to sleep better, but it is also extremely useful for treating irritable stomach activity.
