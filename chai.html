Why Chai Tea?

Chai is an excellent tea to drink for its deliciousness. Hailing from India, Chai tea is a black tea that is steeped in heavy milk and complimented by various spices, such as cinnamon, and a sweetener, such as sugar. While it is not particularly known for its health benefits, chai is a great drink for when one wishes to live luxuriously for an evening.
