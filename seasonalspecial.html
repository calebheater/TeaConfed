What is "Holiday Bliss?"

"Holiday Bliss" is an ideal beverage once you have retreated inside from the nipping winter cold. It is a black tea that is intermixed with peppermint and nutmeg, and is complimented by a hint of valerian root to induce a relaxed sensation without causing excessive drowsiness. Drink a cup with someone close to you this Christmas.
